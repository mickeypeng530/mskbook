## Summary
Comprehensive chapter covering knee MRI technique, meniscal evaluation and tears, cruciate and collateral ligaments, patellofemoral joint, bursae, bone contusions, and soft tissue injuries. Emphasizes practical diagnostic approach with extensive discussion of pitfalls.
